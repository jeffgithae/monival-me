How it works

Three steps from survey submission to indicator update.

  • Connect your platform — link a KoboToolbox, ODK, CommCare, or Ona account, or set up a webhook for push-based platforms.
  • Map your questions — match each survey question to the indicator, activity, or beneficiary field it should populate.
  • Set the sync cadence — choose a sync interval, trigger a sync manually, or let webhook submissions arrive in real time.
  • Review before it counts — synced activities still go through your approval workflow before they affect a reported result.
