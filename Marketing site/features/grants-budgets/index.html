What's tracked

Every figure a grants officer or finance lead actually needs.

  • Budget allocations by fiscal year — categorised line items tied to specific grants or donors.
  • Live burn rate — spent as a percentage of budgeted, calculated automatically, with a running history.
  • Variance tracking — budgeted vs. actual, with variance percentage flagged the moment it moves.
  • Grant periods & compliance deadlines — funder, reporting cadence, and key dates in one record.
  • Approval workflows — budget revisions route through the right reviewers before they're final.
