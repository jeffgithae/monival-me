What it actually does

Three things every M&E officer asks themselves weekly.

  • "Are we ready to report?" — it checks reporting readiness across projects and flags what's holding it back.
  • "What's gone stale?" — it surfaces projects with no recent activity or indicator updates.
  • "What's stuck in review?" — it counts submitted activities still waiting for approval, and tells you that's the fastest lever to pull.
  • "Where are the gaps?" — it points to indicators without recent results or missing evidence.

Why "grounded" matters

It doesn't guess. It reports on what's actually in your workspace.

No hallucinated numbers
