Built for grant performance

NGO monitoring and evaluation software that holds up under donor review.

Evidara gives nonprofit teams a modern alternative to spreadsheet-based M&E. Program managers track logframe indicators, field activities, beneficiaries, and partners while syncing survey data directly from KoboToolbox, ODK, CommCare, or Ona. Finance teams monitor budget and grant variance in the same workspace M&E officers use to validate evidence through configurable, multi-step approval workflows. For NGOs, INGOs, USAID-funded programs, UN sub-recipients, and local implementation partners, Evidara is a reliable system of record for performance management, accountability, and donor reporting — with multi-partner aggregation for consortiums and country networks that need a single rolled-up view.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Evidara only for large NGOs?

No. Starter is built for small teams and pilots; Growth, Organization, and Scale support larger and multi-country portfolios as you grow.

Can Evidara replace our KoboToolbox / ODK workflow?

You don't have to choose. Evidara syncs survey responses directly from KoboToolbox, ODK, CommCare, or Ona, so your field tools stay the same while the data flows straight into your indicators and activities.

Does Evidara support multi-partner or consortium reporting?

Yes — the Scale plan includes network-level aggregation, rolling up results across implementing partners or country offices into one view.

What does the AI copilot actually do?

It answers questions grounded in your real projects, indicators, activities, and reporting periods — like which indicators are off-track or which activities are missing evidence. It does not generate or fabricate data; it only reports on what's already in your workspace.

Is there an audit trail for donor or compliance reviews?

Yes. Every create, edit, approval, and rejection is logged with who, what, and when, available from Growth plan upward.
