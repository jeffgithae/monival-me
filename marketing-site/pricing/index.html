Pricing FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes — every plan starts with a 14-day free trial and no credit card is required to begin.

What's the difference between Starter and Growth?

Starter covers core M&E for up to 5 projects and 10 users. Growth adds grants and budget tracking, approval workflows, the AI copilot, cloud storage sync, and an audit log, with unlimited indicators and room for 20 projects and 50 users.

When do I need the Scale plan?

Scale is built for large INGOs that need SSO/SAML login and multi-partner result aggregation across implementing partners or country offices.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time as your organisation's needs change.
