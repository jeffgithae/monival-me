Summary: We collect only what we need to run the Service. We never sell your data. You own your beneficiary data. We use Paddle for payments and comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019 and the GDPR.

1 Overview

Evidara M&E ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and their beneficiaries. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our Monitoring & Evaluation platform.

We process data in accordance with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable, and other relevant data protection laws.

2 Information We Collect

Account information: Name, email address, organisation name, country, sector, and password (hashed) when you register.

Billing information: Subscription plan and billing history. We do not store card details — all payment data is handled by Paddle.com Market Limited.

Usage data: How you use the Service, features accessed, timestamps, IP addresses, and browser/device information for security and improvement purposes.

Customer Data: All data you enter into the Service — projects, indicators, activities, beneficiary records, grant details, documents, and reports. This data belongs to you.

Communications: If you contact us for support, we retain those communications.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send essential service communications (account verification, security alerts, invoices)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve and develop new features, using anonymised, aggregated data
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not use your data or your beneficiaries' data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to train AI models without your explicit consent.

5 Beneficiary and Programme Data

Evidara M&E is designed for NGOs managing sensitive beneficiary information. We treat this data with heightened care:

  • Beneficiary personal data is stored encrypted and is never accessed by our staff except to resolve technical support issues you have requested
  • We do not analyse, profile, or commercialise beneficiary data
  • Your organisation remains the data controller for all beneficiary data you enter
  • You are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from beneficiaries before entering their data into the platform
Your responsibility: You must ensure you have a lawful basis and appropriate consent to process beneficiary personal data, in line with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and any other applicable law in your operating jurisdiction.

6 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. We implement access controls, authentication (including MFA support), and audit logging to protect your data.

We follow industry best practices for security, including regular security reviews. However, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by applicable law.

7 Data Retention

We retain your account and Customer Data for as long as your subscription is active. On cancellation or termination:

  • You have 30 days to export your data
  • After 30 days, your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely

8 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Restriction: Request we limit processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@evidara.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9 Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the Service to function, including authentication tokens and session management. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

10 International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed outside Kenya, including in the United States and European Union, by our infrastructure providers. Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required by the GDPR.

11 Children's Data

The Service is not directed at children under 18 for account creation. However, NGOs operating child-focused programmes may record data about children as beneficiaries. This must be done in compliance with applicable laws, with appropriate consent from parents or guardians, and in accordance with the principles of data minimisation.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of the Service after that date constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
