Our commitment: We want you to be completely satisfied with Evidara M&E. If the platform doesn't meet your needs, we'll work with you to make it right — including offering a full refund within the eligible period.

1 Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all subscriptions purchased through Evidara M&E. All payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, our Merchant of Record. Refunds are issued through Paddle and returned to the original payment method.

We understand that NGOs operate with tight budgets and donor accountability. We take refund requests seriously and aim to be fair and responsive.

2 Free Trial

We offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. During the trial, you can explore the full functionality of Evidara M&E at no cost.

No refund is needed if you cancel before or during the trial period — you will not be charged.

3 Refund Eligibility

Monthly subscriptions: You are eligible for a full refund if you request it within 7 days of your initial subscription payment or renewal payment, provided you have not extensively used the Service during that period.

Annual subscriptions: You are eligible for a full refund if you request it within 14 days of your annual payment. After 14 days, we may offer a pro-rata refund for unused months at our discretion, particularly where:

  • A documented technical issue prevented you from using the Service
  • The Service materially failed to perform as described
  • Your organisation's funding or programme was cancelled
NGO hardship cases: We recognise that development organisations sometimes face sudden funding cuts or project closures. If your situation falls outside the standard policy, please contact us — we handle these on a case-by-case basis and err on the side of fairness.

4 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us using one of the following methods:

  • Email: billing@evidara.app with subject line "Refund Request"
  • In-app: Navigate to Settings → Billing → Contact Support

Please include in your request:

  • Your registered email address
  • Organisation name
  • Date of payment
  • Reason for the refund request

We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day and resolve it within 5 business days.

5 Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed through Paddle. Once approved:

  • Paddle typically processes the refund within 5–10 business days
  • The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your bank or card issuer, typically an additional 3–7 business days
  • We will email you confirmation once the refund has been issued

6 Cancellation Without Refund

You may cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Billing. After cancellation:

  • Your access continues until the end of the current billing period
  • No further charges will be made
  • No partial refund is issued for the remaining days in the current period (unless within the refund eligibility window above)
  • Your data is retained for 30 days after the end of the billing period, after which it is permanently deleted

7 Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Payments made more than 14 days ago (for annual plans) or 7 days ago (for monthly plans), except in exceptional circumstances
  • Subscriptions where the account has been suspended for violation of our Terms of Service
  • Any add-on services or professional services fees, unless otherwise agreed in writing

8 Contact

For refund requests or billing questions:

  • Email: billing@evidara.app
  • General support: hello@evidara.app

Payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited. For payment disputes not resolved by us, you may also contact Paddle directly at support@paddle.com.
